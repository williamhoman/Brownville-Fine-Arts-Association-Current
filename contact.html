2016 Special Events

For a town of less than 150 people, we still keep pretty active! Check out our community calendar to see up-to-date events going on in town. Click HERE to download our 2016 Calendar of Events brochure.

Annual Events

The Brownville Concert Series - The Series offers nationally recognized performers presenting concerts and cabarets monthly during the months of March through December. Call 402.825.3331 or visit www.BrownvilleConcertSeries.com.
Spirit of Brownville - Coming to town on a weekend between Memorial Day & Labor Day? Be sure to reserve a spot on the sightseeing and dinner cruise aboard the Spirit. Cruises run every Friday, Saturday & Sunday between Memorial Day and Labor Day starting at 5:30. For reservations or information call 402.825.6441 or visit www.River-Inn-Resort.com.
59th Annual Brownville Spring Flea Market - May 28-30, 2016. Over 300 vendors arrive with antiques, plants, collectibles and food. Visit www.BrownvilleHistoricalSociety.org for more infomation.
Whiskey Run Creek Friday Night Summer Music Series - Every Friday night from May 1 through August 31st swing by the winery to celebrate the region's musical heritage. Performers change each week so check the calendar or Facebook to see who is playing each week. No advance reservations required. Call 402.825.4601 or visit www.whiskeyruncreek.com.
Brownville Village Theatre - The oldest repertory theatre in Nebraska offers four plays and a musical all summer long. Call 402.825.4121 or visit www.BrownvilleVillageTheatre.org for more information and showtimes.
Annual Furnas Arboretum Garden Tour - Activities are scheduled throughout the day, featuring Nebraska Statewide Arboretum representatives. The date for 2016 is June 11 from 10 am to 4 pm. Call 402.825.6637 for more information.
Freedom Day Celebration - Celebrate our nation's Independence in Brownville! We start the day with a 5k, 10k, 1/2 marathon run and continue the day with children's activities, dog show, parade and river boat cruises. Finish off the day with a concert aboard the River Inn and enjoy fireworks over the Mighty Missouri River. For more information on the Freedom Run, click HERE. Click HERE for the full schedule for the Freedom Day Celebration activities
Brownville Historical Society Golf Classic - Four person teams, scramble with a shot gun start. The date for 2016 is August 27th at the Auburn Country Club. Call 402.825.3591 for more information.
58th Annual Brownville Fall Flea Market - September 24 & 25, 2016. More of what you love...300+ vendors. Visit www.BrownvilleHistoricalSociety.org for more infomation.
Old Time Autumn - River boat cruises, hay rack rides, craftsmen, artisans, buggy rides, food, entertainment & more. The dates for 2016 are October 8 & 9. Call 402.825.6001 or visit www.BrownvilleHistoricalSociety.org for more information.
A Brownville Christmas - Shopping, lights & more. The date for 2016 is December 3rd. Call 402.825.6001 or visit www.BrownvilleHistoricalSociety.org for more information.
First Day of Winter Celebration - Kick off winter by running a 5k that benefits the Auburn BackPack Program. Later that evening you can enjoy a musician in the warmth of the barn loft. The date for 2016 is December 17th. Call 402.825.4601 or visit www.whiskeyruncreek.com for more information.
